Pesto Scrambled Eggs Recipe



Description

Pesto scrambled eggs are so delicious! Serve on toast with a few slices of bacon on the side. You'llfind a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:


Ingredients

  • ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • ½ teaspoon pesto

Steps

  1. Combine Cheddar, egg, salt, and pepper in a small bowl; whisk until well combined.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Pour egg mixture into the skillet; cook and stir until eggs are set, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and stir in pesto.
